HATSE 2024

Welcome to the website for the HATSE 2024 conference!

The VT Conference on Hokies in Advanced Topics in Software Engineering (HATSE) is the premier software enginering conference in Blacksburg, VA. HATSE brings together expert researchers from Virginia Tech to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, experiences, and challenges related to humans in the field of software engineering.

The main conference will be held TBD. This website is under development, and more information will be added in the coming months.
